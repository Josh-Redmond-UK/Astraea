Welcome

This web page contains information about my PhD project, "Participatory Design of Citizen-led Remote Sensing Forensics Tools in Mexico" that I worked on at the University of Exeter from 2019-2024. The purpose of this page is to collect basic information about the project into one place, so that others can see what it was about and make use of some of the outputs for themselves.

Supervised by Ernesto Schwartz-Marin, Hugo Barbosa, and Ana Beduschi, I carried out participatory design research to develop new tools for satellite image forensics and analysis, primarily based on Google Earth Engine.
